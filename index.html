Chicken Biryani Recipe

    I am going to make chicken biryani

    Ingredients

  • Chicken 1 kg
  • Basmati Rice 1 kg
  • Oil 500 grm
  • Tomato 300 grm
  • Onion 300 grm
  • Adrak 200 grm
  • Dhaniya 200 grm
  • Podeena 200 grm
  • Salt need basis
  • Garam Masala
  • Green Chilli need basis
  • Zarda Color need basis

    Method

  1. First of all put the Rice in some water for 20 minutes
  2. Wash the chicken properly and cut in pieces as you like
  3. Grind the Tomato, Onion, Dhaniya, Adrak and Podeena and make the pase
  4. Puth the degee on burner and put some water in it to boil
  5. on boiling the water put the rice in it and keep it on slow burner
  6. put the chicken in a Karahi
  7. put the paste of onion, tomato etc in the Chicken and keep it on slow burner
  8. Keep the chicken for 20 minutes upto brownish
  9. After boil the rice the water may be fell down
  10. After cooks the chicken it may be applied in rice by part with the Zarda color
  11. yes my Chicken Biryani is ready to eat please
